About SAPYK

Discover our roots, our philosophy, and the journey of Sri Atmananda Pranayama Yoga Kendra.

Our Philosophy

At Sri Atmananda Pranayama Yoga Kendra, we believe that true wellness comes from the harmony of mind, body, and spirit. Our teachings are rooted in ancient traditions, adapted for the modern seeker.

We focus heavily on Pranayama (breath control) as the gateway to deep meditation and physical vitality. Through dedicated practice, our students find profound peace and resilience against the stresses of daily life.

  • Authentic teachings passed down through generations.
  • A welcoming community for practitioners of all levels.
  • Holistic approach integrating diet, lifestyle, and practice.
